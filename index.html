PAWNCH GYM OPEN

半夜也開著的拳館。 A boxing gym that stays open late.

Pawnch 是一個拳擊間歇計時器 app。貓咪館長 Miso 顧櫃檯，不推銷、不喊口號，只記得你有沒有來，也不會因為你沒來就唸你。 Pawnch is a boxing interval timer app. Miso, the gym cat, mans the front desk — no sales pitch, no hype, just quietly keeping track of whether you showed up — and won't lecture you if you didn't.

Features

拳館裡有的東西 What's in the gym

沒有等級、沒有排行榜、沒有推播騷擾。就是一間你可以隨時進來打幾拳的地方。 No levels, no leaderboards, no push notifications nagging you. Just a place you can walk into any time and throw a few punches.

⏱️

回合間歇計時Round Timer

自訂回合數、時間、休息長度。準備、出拳、休息、收操——聲音跟震動會提醒你，不用一直盯螢幕。 Set your own rounds, durations, and rest length. Get ready, throw punches, rest, cool down — sound and vibration cue every phase, so you never have to stare at the screen.

🐾

出席簿Attendance Log

每次練完蓋一枚出席章。不是連續打卡壓力，只是老實記錄你這個月來了幾個晚上。 Every session stamps your attendance log. No streak pressure — just an honest record of how many nights you showed up this month.

🐱

Miso 顧櫃檯Miso, the Gym Cat

撲克臉、半睡半醒、講話不多。會依時段、依你多久沒來，換一句招呼——僅止於此，不推銷。 Poker-faced, half-asleep, a cat of few words. His greeting changes with the time of day and how long you've been away — that's it, no sales pitch attached.

📌

收藏櫃 & 留言板Collection & Message Board

常來會慢慢解鎖拳館裡的舊物件；留言板是 Miso 隨手貼的紙條，有空再翻，不是通知中心。 Keep coming back and you'll slowly unlock old gym relics. The message board is just notes Miso leaves lying around — flip through when you feel like it, it's not a notification center.

Honest Facts

老實說清楚 The honest facts

這是一個工具 app，不是要留住你的產品。以下是它實際上會（不會）做的事。 This is a tool, not a product designed to hook you. Here's exactly what it does — and doesn't do.

  • 無帳號、無登入。打開就能用，不用註冊、不用綁 email。
  • No account, no login. Open the app and go — no sign-up, no email required.
  • 無追蹤 SDK。沒有第三方分析，沒有人在看你怎麼用這個 app。
  • No tracking SDKs. No third-party analytics — nobody's watching how you use this app.
  • 資料只在你的手機裡。不上傳、不備份到我們的伺服器（我們也沒有伺服器）。
  • Your data stays on your phone. Nothing is uploaded or backed up to our servers — because we don't have any.
  • 繁體中文＋英文。右上角可切換語言，選定後介面文字不會忽然跳來跳去。
  • Traditional Chinese + English. Pick a language and the whole interface sticks with it — no mid-sentence switching.
  • 下載免費，唯一的內購是自願打賞。「請 Miso 吃罐罐」——付款全部經過 Apple StoreKit，我們不經手你的付款資訊。
  • Free to download, the only IAP is an optional tip. "Treat Miso to a Can" — all payments go through Apple StoreKit, and we never handle your payment info.
