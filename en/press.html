中文

Pawnch 是一款拳擊間歇計時器，館長是一隻叫 Miso 的橘白貓。他會記得你有沒有來練，但不會逼你、不會扣分，也不會在你休息一天後跳出來碎念，只是安靜開著燈等你進門。同樣適合居家 HIIT、有氧格鬥與循環訓練，回合鈴聲鎖屏也照響。

English

Pawnch is a boxing interval timer run by Miso, an orange-and-white gym cat who's a little lazy, a little messy, and always remembers whether you showed up. Miso doesn't nag, doesn't dock points, and won't guilt-trip you over a broken streak — the lights are just always on, waiting for you to walk in. Built for boxing, kickboxing, and home HIIT, Pawnch keeps the round bell ringing even when the screen is locked, with no account, no login, and no tracking required to train.
